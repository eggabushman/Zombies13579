Surroundings:

Know How to Effectively Utilize your Environment to Survive


The Design Den

The Design Den is the largest source of supplies and tools in the school. With its huge supply of metal and wood, the Design Den will be very useful when constructing fortifications.

The Roof

The Roof is an excellent place to post sentries and monitor the surrounding land. It is also an optimal location for a sign to attract rescue aircraft, supply drops, or government support (Ex: “HELP”, “S.O.S”, or “SURVIVORS INSIDE”).

The Weight Room & Gym

The Weight Room is a source of lots of craftable metal if needed. More importantly, staying fit is necessary to outlast the zombie horde and the Gym must be used accordingly.

The Black Box Theater & Music Rooms

The Black Box Theater is yet another location for wood supplies and manual tools. It is also a viable place to boost morale with performances. Additionally, instruments are a wonderful, and often overlooked way to relieve stress and keep your mind occupied on something other than the undead for once.

The Creek & Greenspace

These outdoor areas serve as potential farmland and a source of running water that may require extra purification.

The Kitchen & PAWS Store

The Kitchen is first a source of perishable and nonperishable food and drink. The perishable goods should be consumed first before they expire. Tools and weapons can also be made from standard kitchen utensils. The PAWS store is also a more limited source of nonperishable food and drink, and an additional source of entertainment.

The Library (& some Classrooms)

The Library/Classrooms can serve as sources of entertainment and mostly research. Books have the potential to maintain and improve morale, sanity, and mental acuity among the survivors.

The City

It is always of utmost importance to gain a heightened awareness of your surroundings. After securing and establishing your home base, you should look to nearby stores and residences for your designated scavenging parties to find more gear and building materials. This is how you will survive in the longterm.
